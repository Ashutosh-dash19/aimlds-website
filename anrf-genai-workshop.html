ANRF GenAI Workshop

Exploring Future Research Developments with Generative AI Technology Innovations

About the Workshop

The ANRF Sponsored National Workshop on Generative AI brought together academicians, researchers, and students to explore the emerging opportunities in artificial intelligence. It focused on the transformative impact of GenAI on industries and research domains.

The sessions covered key areas including large language models, prompt engineering, ethical considerations, and hands-on demonstrations of AI tools. Renowned experts shared valuable insights into the role of GenAI in reshaping education, business, and research ecosystems.

Participants gained practical exposure through interactive sessions, group discussions, and case studies. This workshop provided a platform for collaboration, innovation, and knowledge exchange, empowering students to embrace AI-driven solutions in their academic and professional journeys.
