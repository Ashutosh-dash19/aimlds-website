Overview

The B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning is a four-year undergraduate programme designed to provide strong foundations in programming, data structures, algorithms, AI, and deep learning. It nurtures critical thinking and prepares students for research, innovation, and industry challenges.

Why Choose This Course?

  • Hands-on learning with AI labs & real projects
  • Industry-driven curriculum aligned with global trends
  • Research opportunities with expert faculty
  • Internship & placement support with top companies

What You Will Learn

  • Programming in Python, Java, C++
  • Data Science, ML, and Deep Learning
  • Computer Vision & Natural Language Processing
  • Big Data & Cloud Computing
  • Entrepreneurship & research methodology

Career Prospects

Graduates can pursue careers as Data Scientists, AI Engineers, Software Developers, and Researchers in both academia and industry. They can also explore entrepreneurship or higher studies in specialized fields of AI and ML.
