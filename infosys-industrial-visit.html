Infosys Mysore Industrial Visit

A glimpse into innovation, technology, and corporate culture at one of the world’s leading IT campuses.

About the Visit

The Department of AIML & Data Science organized an enriching industrial visit to the Infosys Campus in Mysore, one of the largest corporate training facilities in the world. This visit provided students with firsthand exposure to a global IT environment and modern infrastructure.

Students learned about Infosys’ training methodologies, corporate work culture, and technological innovations. The interactive sessions highlighted how the company nurtures talent and prepares employees for dynamic global challenges.

The visit not only bridged the gap between academic learning and industry practices but also inspired students to align their skills with real-world applications. It was an eye-opening experience into the life and opportunities of working at a top IT firm like Infosys.
