Hackathons

Innovating, collaborating, and solving real-world challenges through coding marathons.

Why Hackathons Matter

Hackathons at CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Department of AIML & Data Science, provide students with a platform to showcase creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. These coding sprints push boundaries, encouraging participants to design impactful AI-powered solutions within a limited timeframe.

Participants collaborate in teams, brainstorm innovative ideas, and translate concepts into working prototypes. Hackathons foster not just coding expertise but also leadership, time management, and entrepreneurial thinking.

From university-level challenges to national and international competitions, our students actively participate, learn, and compete with the best. These experiences prepare them for industry roles and research-driven futures.
