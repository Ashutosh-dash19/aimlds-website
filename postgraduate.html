Overview

M. Tech in Data Science is a two year, four semester programme aimed at imparting knowledge in data science, machine learning, statistics, visualization, and more. It prepares students for regional, national, and international needs in AI, NLP, and data-driven innovation.

Why Choose This Course?

  • Dedicated Data Science & Analytics Labs
  • Industry 4.0 curriculum aligned with trends
  • Global exposure via student exchange programmes
  • Fasttrack completion option
  • 85% PhD-qualified faculty
  • Industry tie-ups for placements & internships

What You Will Learn

  • Apply mathematical & data science methods to real problems
  • Data visualization with SPSS & Tableau
  • Web technologies: HTML, CSS, JS, backend
  • Problem-solving using ML & engineering techniques
  • Research writing & professional practices

Modules

Year 1: DS & Algorithms, Databases, Data Mining, Visualization, Optimization, Big Data, Research Methodology

Year 2: Program Elective, Global Elective, Dissertation, Internship

Career Prospects

Graduates can work in academia, industries, and research labs with roles in AI, ML, NLP, Big Data, and Data Visualization. Opportunities extend to entrepreneurship and global data-driven innovation.
